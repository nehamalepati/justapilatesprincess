from first light to moonlight

six signature classes that follow the arc of a day — and the arc of a woman's life. every one of them lives by the same rule: you can breathe slowly, find your safe neutral, take your time, and still get an incredible workout. the flows are written mat-first so you can practice anywhere — but i'm comprehensively certified, so in a private any of them can move to the reformer, chair, or tower.

sunrise · start the day

morning mobility

ten-to-twenty minute wake-up flows for joints and spine — the moving version of your morning chai. perfect before work or after school drop-off.

the signature flow  ·  bharatanatyam × pilates

desi-lates flows — one practice, three depths

my own invention — the storytelling of classical south indian dance fused with the movement principles of pilates. these classes run at three levels: start exactly where you are, and the story deepens as you go.

01

beginner · learn the language

aramandi (the half-sit), your first adavu footwork, and hasta hand positions — layered over breath and a neutral spine. no dance background needed.
