The Practice

no experience needed, crowns provided

No power flows, no pressure. Every class I teach lives in one of three homes — on the mat or the full studio equipment — and all of them center women's health, from your first-ever class to pregnancy, postpartum, and beyond

నాట్యం

Desi-lates Flows

My own invention — the storytelling of classical South Indian dance (Bharatanatyam) fused with the movement principles of pilates. You'll move with intention, learn natya & abhinaya, and still get a full-body workout

ఆరోగ్యం

Restorative for Beginners

If pilates is brand new to you — mat or machine — start here. Gentle, fully-guided sessions that build confidence and core awareness — modifications always offered, never an afterthought

ప్రాణాయామం

Stretch & Recovery

Deep, slow stretching for tired bodies — desk shoulders, runner hips, new-mom backs. Long holds, deep breaths, and permission to do less

Credentials & Kind Words

every princess needs her practice

i have always believed that there is no such thing as a “pilates body” — Any body that practices pilates is a pilates body

just a pilates princess was born from that feeling, the idea that anyone can be a pilates princess and feel beautiful connecting with their mind, body, and spirit

i hope you'll follow me on my journey, and pick up your crown along the way

Balanced Body Comprehensive Certified

Full comprehensive instructor training — mat, reformer, chair, tower & barrels — plus anatomy and programming for every body.

NPCP Pre & Postnatal Certified

Specialized certification for teaching safely through pregnancy and postpartum recovery — the heart of the women's health work I do.
