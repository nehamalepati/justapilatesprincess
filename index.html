The Practice

Movement that meets you where you are

No power flows, no pressure. Every class I teach lives in one of three homes — and all of them center women's health, from your first-ever class to pregnancy, postpartum, and beyond.

Desi-lates Flows

My own invention — the storytelling of classical South Indian dance (Bharatanatyam) fused with the movement principles of pilates. You'll move with intention, learn natya & abhinaya, and still get a full-body workout.

Restorative for Beginners

If you've never stepped on a mat, start here. Gentle, fully-guided sessions that build confidence and core awareness — modifications always offered, never an afterthought.

Stretch & Recovery

Deep, slow stretching for tired bodies — desk shoulders, runner hips, new-mom backs. Long holds, deep breaths, and permission to do less.
